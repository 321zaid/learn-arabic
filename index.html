State 03 — The Prayer Connection

The Prayer Connection

You say this in every single prayer:

ٱلسَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ ٱللَّٰهِ وَبَرَكَاتُهُ

ٱلسَّلَامُ (As-Salamu) comes directly from S-L-M. Every time you say it, you are literally declaring "The Peace" — one of Allah's beautiful names, built from the exact same three letters you learned today.
